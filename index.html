FAQs

What is HackBCA?

HackBCA is the first high school hackathon, hosted at the Bergen County Acadmies, Hackensack, New Jersey. A hackathon is when people who are passionate about something come together for a weekend and collaborate to create cool things.

Do I need to code?

No, in this hackathon you can do anything from making music to making a robot.

Who can come?

Any high schooler from anywhere in the world. Contact us for more information.

How do I get there?

We will provide a travel reimbursement of up to $200.

What should I bring?

You should bring your Laptop, charger, cell phone and cell phone charger, and power strips if you have them



What are we providing?

There will be food, bathrooms, if you have allergies let us know.

When can you start coding?

You have to start when you the event starts, you are not allowed to start beforehand.

When do you submit your app?

You must submit your app through a link on the day of the hackathon.

How does Judging work?

There will be a committee of 6-8 judges who will vote on which apps win.

Do you need a team beforehand?

No you do not, you can find one here or work individually.

Will you have fun?

Of course it will be the best highschool hackathon ever!
