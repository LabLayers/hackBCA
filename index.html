FAQ


What is hackBCA?

hackBCA is one of the first ever high school hackathons! It will be hosted at the Bergen County Acadmies High School in Hackensack, New Jersey.

When can I start coding?

I know you are eager to start your awesome project, but you must wait until the hackathon starts (sorry).

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event in which teams spend the weekend building something awesome (and then demo it). hackBCA isn't your traditional hackathon, we also allow (and encourage) not just software devs to attend, but also mucisians, engineers, scientists, etc.

I'm not in high school anymore, can I still come and watch the demos?

Definitely! You can get a ticket for the demos below:

How and when do I submit my hack?

You must submit your hack by 10:00am on Sunday, but can still work on them after 10. We will send an email with more information on Sunday.

Do I need to code or like computers?

Nope! In fact, you don't even have to be a developer. You can do anything from making a song to designing and making a hat. All tangible projects are premitted (sorry, no business plans).

What should I bring?

You should bring your laptop (we won't provide computers) & charger, phone & charger, and power strips (recommended).

How does judging work?

On Sunday, you will be given the chance to demo your project. The judges will then choose winners for our prizes, and our sponsors will choose the winners for their prizes.

Who can attend?

Any student still in high school (or younger) can attend! You don't have to be from NJ (or even from the US).

Will you reimburse my travel expenses?

Yes! Contact us for more information at contact@hackBCA.com.

Do I need a team beforehand?

Nope, you can find one here or work individually.

Will it be awesome?

It will be awesome.


If you have any other questions, don't hesitate to contact us at contact@hackBCA.com
